Improved version of the 1.5 inch aluminium gridbeam set (https://3dwarehouse.sketchup.com/model/59a44de0b3f5694a4ae5e8dc149c74c/15-aluminum-gridbeam-set )
All gridbeam versions (1 inch, 1.5 inch and 2 inch) are accurately modeled herein.
For 1 inch gridbeam that means: hole size= 11/32 inch, bolt size=5/16 inch
For 1.5 inch gridbeam that means: hole size= 11/32 inch, bolt size=5/16 inch
For 2 inch gridbeam that means: hole size= 7/16 inch, bolt size=3/18 inch

Length of the gridbeams is per 1 foot and per 0.5 foot (upto a maximum length of 8 foot)
