Design of a tadpole tricycle, which is able to carry freight (groceries, ...)
Steering is done with the beam (handlebar) on top of the container box.

Bill Of Materials (metric, all 40 mm GB except the 0,7m and 1,5m beams which are 50 mm ) 
7x 0,5 m gridbeam
2x 0,6 m gridbeam
6x 0,8 m gridbeam
1x 0,7 m gridbeam (50 mm)
1x 1,5 m gridbeam (50 mm)
2x 406 mm wheel
1x 622 mm wheel
